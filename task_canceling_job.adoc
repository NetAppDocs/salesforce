= Canceling a job
:toc: macro
:toclevels: 1
:hardbreaks:
:nofooter:
:icons: font
:linkattrs:
:imagesdir: ./media/

[.lead]
You can cancel an immediate backup or restore.  You cannot cancel a scheduled backup.

toc::[]

.Steps

. Click *Jobs* on the left navigation pane.
. Under *Recent Running Jobs*, find the job that you want to cancel.
. Click *Cancel*.
