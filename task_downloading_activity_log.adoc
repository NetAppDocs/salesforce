= Downloading the activity log
:toc: macro
:toclevels: 1
:hardbreaks:
:nofooter:
:icons: font
:linkattrs:
:imagesdir: ./media/

[.lead]
You can download the activity log to a .csv file.

.Steps

. From the left-pane navigation, click *Reporting*.
  image:reporting.jpg[]
.	Click *Activity Log*.
. Click *Download*.
  image:download.jpg[]
  The activity log is downloaded as a .csv file.
